About Us

Miss Face of Courageous South Africa is more than just a beauty pageant â€” it is a movement dedicated to empowering young women to become leaders of purpose, compassion, and community impact. Founded on the values of courage, humanity, and service, our pageant celebrates queens who wear their crowns with responsibility and heart.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Who can enter Miss Face of Courageous South Africa?

The pageant is open to young women ages 4 to 29 from all nine provinces of South Africa. We proudly embrace diversity and inclusivity, welcoming contestants from every cultural and community background.

2. Do I need prior pageant experience to enter?

No. We welcome both beginners and experienced contestants. Our focus is on growth, confidence, and purpose.

3. What qualities are you looking for in a contestant?

We look for young women who show courage, compassion, and a willingness to serve others. Contestants should be passionate about helping the needy, giving back to their community, and finding and performing their purpose and advocacy.

5. Where and how are workshops held?

All workshops are done online, making them accessible to contestants across South Africa.

6. Where will the finale event take place?

The event is hosted in a different province each year. For this year, the finale will be held at Khoroni Hotel in Venda, Limpopo.

7. What activities do contestants participate in?

Contestants take part in:

  • Online workshops and training
  • Community outreach and charity projects
  • Interviews
  • Leadership development
  • Advocacy development
  • Pageant rehearsals and the final event
8. Is the pageant only about beauty?

No. Our pageant is built on courage, compassion, purpose, and community impact. True beauty is how you uplift others.

9. Will contestants receive support for their advocacy projects?

Yes. We guide contestants in discovering, planning, and performing their purpose and advocacy, ensuring they create meaningful change in their communities.
